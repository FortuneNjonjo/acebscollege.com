About Us

Founded in 2018, Africa Centre for Business Studies (ACEBS) is a private institution based in Mvuma, Zimbabwe. We are dedicated to producing ethical, innovative, and competent business leaders across Africa.

Our mission is to provide accessible, world-class education that bridges the gap between academic theory and business practice. We are registered with the Ministry of Higher and Tertiary Education, Science and Technology Development.
